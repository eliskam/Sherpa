Welcome to Sherpa

Never drag your bag again.

Sherpa is an on-demand luggage delivery service securely moving your bags between homes and airports, stations.

Step One: Book a collection

In just a few clicks, book your Sherpa to collect your bags from your home, hotel or airbnb (or boat if you're that cool!)

Step Two: Secure bag collection

At your desired time and location, your Sherpa will arrive to collect your bags, secure them with our tamper-free technology and safely store them until you need them back.
